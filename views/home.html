Welcome to the Kenneth Batcher Memorial Website!

A memorial site for Professor Kenneth E. Batcher developed by Kent State CS Students. Professor Batcher was a beloved professor at Kent State University who has made significant contributions to the Computer Science Field. From academics, looking to reference Batcher's work, to upcoming students interested in learning more about Professor Batcher, we welcome all users!

Academic Research

Looking for Batcher's work to reference for your research? Visit the archive page to dive deeper into references. You can also visit the algorithms page to learn more about Batcher's Odd-Even Mergesort and Bitonic Mergesort Algorithms.

Learn more about Professor Batcher

Want to learn more about Batcher's life and accomplishments? Visit the biography page for an in depth view. We have also made sure to preserve Batcher's Faculty Page from when he taught at Kent State University.
